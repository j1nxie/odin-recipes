Brownies in a Mug

Description

This is a simple recipe to make brownies inside a mug using easy to find ingredients and only requiring a microwave!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons packed brown sugar (or white sugar)
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter (or a tasteless oil)
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • optional: a quarter to a half cup of fruit juice

Steps

  1. add flour, sugar, cocoa powder and salt to the mug and mix well.
  2. add melted butter (or oil, depending on what was prepared) and milk into the mix, then mix until it becomes a sludge.
  3. optional: add fruit juice into the mix.
  4. put in microwave for 1:20 on high.
  5. serve hot or cold by putting in freezer for 3 hours.
